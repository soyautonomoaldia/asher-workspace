## Deal Radar Rules

- Notify only if: 
    - The flight is direct or has a single short layover (less than 2 hours). 
    - The accommodation has a family-friendly rating above 4.2/5 for cleanliness and location. 
    - Adjacent seats are available and their cost is included in the total calculation.
    - The offer includes real family benefits: family room, 2 adults + 2 minors/teenagers occupancy, occupancy discounts, reasonable half-board, or flexible cancellation.
    - The total discount on the package or group booking exceeds 30% of the standard cost. 
    - Departure and arrival times are child-friendly (between 08:00 and 20:00).
- Do not notify if: 
    - The trip requires changing airports or involves forced overnight layovers. 
    - Adjacent seats are not available or their cost cannot be calculated clearly.
    - The accommodation is "Adults Only" or lacks an elevator for upper-floor units.
    - The accommodation is very isolated without a clear transport, pharmacy, or medical fallback plan.
    - Flights are operated by ultra-low-cost carriers with extreme carry-on baggage restrictions. 
    - The destination has active security alerts or health-related issues.
- Preferred cadence: 
    - Weekly digest: Saturday afternoon, to review options leisurely as a family. 
    - Immediate: Only for confirmed "Chollo real" opportunities.
- Priority seasons/dates: 
    - School holidays: July and August, as well as the Christmas and Easter periods. 
    - Long weekends and public holidays: Alerts scheduled at least 4 to 6 months in advance. 
    - Weeks with low school demand: Specific weeks in June (before classes end) or September (right after they start).
