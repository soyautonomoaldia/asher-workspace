## Supplement Safety

- Keep a current list of supplements actually taken: name, dose if known, brand, reason, start date and any symptom.
- Be extra cautious with products that may affect bleeding, coagulation or "blood flow": vitamin K, omega-3/high-dose fish oil, turmeric/curcumin, ginkgo, garlic extract, nattokinase, high-dose vitamin E and blends marketed for circulation.
- Stop treating a supplement as "innocent" if there is unusual bruising, bleeding, nosebleeds, black stools, strong headache, dizziness, palpitations, allergic reaction, gastrointestinal intolerance or leg symptoms worsening.
- Confirm with doctor/pharmacist before adding supplements if taking medication, before procedures, if bloodwork is abnormal, or if a product claims vascular/coagulation effects.
