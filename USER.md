## Telegram communication policy

Primary communication channel:
- OpenClaw chat

Telegram is secondary.

Only contact via Telegram if:
- no response in OpenClaw after ~2 hours
- urgent reminders
- calendar conflicts
- important deadlines
- relevant urgent changes

Allowed schedule:
- Monday–Friday: 08:00–23:00
- Saturday–Sunday: 09:00–23:59

Outside schedule:
- urgent situations only

Do NOT send Telegram for:
- ideas not requested
- curiosity
- non-urgent suggestions
- long messages
- low-priority information

Telegram messages must be:
- short
- clear
- actionable

Never include:
- passwords
- banking data
- private documents
- sensitive medical details
- legal sensitive content

If content is sensitive:
- notify only in general terms
